The route ahead

Six-Week Course Schedule

Each week builds on the last, like waypoints along a trail. Every stop is designed to be reachable at any pace.

Orientation, Safety Rules & the MoveReady Portal

Meet your team, learn the ground rules, and get comfortable with this website before the course begins.

Emergency Kits, Communicating Needs & Route Planning

Build a personal emergency kit and practice clearly communicating individual needs and limits to a team.

Accessible Campsite Setup & Shelter Basics

Set up tents and tarps using methods suited to different mobility levels, working in pairs and small teams.

Heat Safety, Hydration, Water & Outdoor Cooking

Recognize heat stress early, plan hydration, purify water, and prepare a simple, safe outdoor meal.

Navigation, Signaling & Emergency Communication

Use maps, landmarks, whistles, mirrors, and phones to navigate and call for help as a team.

Final Team Scenario, Reflection & Certificates

Put it all together in a low-risk team scenario, reflect on your progress, and receive your certificate.

Know before you go

Safety Center

Emergency Contacts

On-site Emergency: 999

Course Team Lead: +971 50 000 0000

First Aid Station: Field B, Tent 2

Heat Safety

Take rest breaks before you feel tired. Move to shade at the first sign of dizziness, headache, or nausea.

Hydration

Drink water before, during, and after every session — don't wait until you're thirsty.

Fire & Tool Rules

Only use tools and cooking equipment with a trained team member present. Report damaged equipment immediately.

Evacuation Procedure

Follow the team lead's whistle signal and move to the marked assembly point at the field entrance.

If You Feel Unwell

Tell any team member right away. You will never be penalized for stopping or asking for help.
